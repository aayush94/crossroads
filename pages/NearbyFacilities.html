Nearby Facilities

If you are looking for a bite to eat, there is a little something for every palate within close proximity. If you are pressed for time and need to grab something fast to eat, there is a Subway, McDonald’s, and a Mr. Sub. If you are looking for a nice restaurant to sit and enjoy a meal, Humpty’s Family Restaurant, The Coliseum Restaurant, Brother’s Family Restaurant, Homesteader Chinese Restaurant, Silver Dragon Chinese Restaurant, and an A&W Restaurant are all located nearby as well. If you are in the mood for pizza, there is a Panago Pizza and Western Pizza as well.

The Kindersley District Hospital, as well as the Kindersley Clinic are both located just a short 0.8Km away for any urgent medical needs that may arise. There is also a dental clinic nearby as well.

Nearby businesses include a Walmart, Home Hardware, and Co-Op Hardware. The Sask Liquor Board Store and an Extra Foods supermarket are also conveniently close by.
